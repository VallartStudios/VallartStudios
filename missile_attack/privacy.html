Privacy Policy

Missile Attack protects your privacy as best as possible.

Information we collect

We only collect data necessary for the game work properly and to make it at least a bit profitable with advertisement and in-app products.

We collect information in three ways:

  • Missile Attack itself:

    Your progress in the levels, coins amount, game objects that you bought and any other game related data are saved on your device local storage.

  • Unity Analytics:

    Unity Analytics is used to help us provide in-app purchases. Learn more about which data is collected in Unity Privacy Policy.

  • Google Admob our advertisement partner:

    The Mobile Ads system of Google Admob collects and shares anonymous data automatically. More information about Admob and Google's policies.

How do we use your information

  • Missile Attack itself:

    As previously said, we only use your data to make sure that our game works and we do not use them in any other way.

  • Unity Analytics:

    Find all the details about how collected data is used in Unity Privacy Policy.

  • Google Admob our advertisement partner:

    Data is used locally on your device for technical purpose and shared over the network to Google Admob for personalized advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention purposes. All of the data collected is encrypted in transit using the Transport Layer Security (TLS) protocol. More information about Admob and Google's policies.

Changes

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time and we will post any privacy policy changes on this page.
